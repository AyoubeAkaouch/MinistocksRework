Ministocks is a simple and powerful stocks widget, giving you quick and easy access to your stock information.

The Ministocks stock widget has the following features:

  • Four widget sizes available
  • Add unlimited widgets
  • Stock symbol search
  • Price change - daily and against buy price
  • Profit and loss - current valuation and total gain
  • High price and low price alerts
  • Foreign exchange prices
  • Data sourced from Yahoo Finance
  • Foreign exchange daily changes
  • Custom update schedule

View Ministocks on Google Play

Ministocks uses finance data sourced from Yahoo Finance, and recognises the same symbols (note that some symbols may not be available).
